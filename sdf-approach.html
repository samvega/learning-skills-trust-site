Our approach

The PSB philosophy, extended to Year 11

The SDF carries the skills-based thinking of the PSB into secondary school — a modern pilot built for how young people learn, work and grow today, and for the demands they will meet beyond school.

  • Extends trusted skills assessment from Year 9 through Year 11
  • Designed around today's classrooms and tomorrow's demands
  • A shared language of skills that bridges prep and senior school

What it develops

Skills for a fast-moving world

Adaptability

Confidence to learn, unlearn and relearn as technology and work keep changing.

Critical thinking

Reasoning, evaluating and problem-solving — the human skills machines can't replace.

Self-direction

Independence, resilience and ownership of one's own learning and choices.
