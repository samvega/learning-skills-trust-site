How it works

Measuring more than marks

The PSB is a programme of study that actively develops and tracks knowledge, skills and attitudes — giving a fuller, more honest record of a child than results alone.

  • Exit points at 11+ and 13+
  • Skills assessed and reported alongside academic progress
  • A shared language of achievement across the whole school
11+ & 13+exit points
Rigour you can trust

Moderated, and recognised

What’s assessed

Knowledge, skills and attitudes — developed and evidenced across subjects, not bolted on.

How it’s moderated

Through annual subject meetings, teachers across PSB schools have the opportunity to moderate and advise one another — keeping standards consistent and rigorous.

Recognised by seniors

Most senior schools accept the PSB certificate at 11+/13+ alongside their own tests or ISEB papers. Our strong links with senior schools also provide valuable insight into the skills and attributes pupils need as they progress.

Interested in the PSB?

Our Director of Schools, Gregg Davies, is happy to talk through membership and how the PSB could work in your school.
