Why now

Preparing young people for a world that keeps moving

The pace of change — in technology, work and society — has never been faster. The SDF gives secondary schools a modern, structured way to develop the human skills that will matter most: adaptability, critical thinking, communication and self-direction.

  • Extends trusted skills assessment from Year 9 through Year 11
  • Designed around today's classrooms and tomorrow's demands
  • A shared language of skills that bridges prep and senior school
