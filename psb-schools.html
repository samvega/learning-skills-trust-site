Senior & associate schools

The PSB's relationship with senior schools is fundamental to its success. Associate senior schools provide staff for subject meetings, host meetings and conferences, and help shape the project. Over 50 senior schools now accept the PSB for entry at 11+/13+ alongside their own requirements.

Prospective members

Joining the PSB

A programme for private and state schools with exit points at 11+ and 13+, preparing young adults for senior school academically, personally, socially, culturally and spiritually — with member schools collaborating and sharing resources throughout.

A complete framework

Distinct from the International Baccalaureate, the PSB gives a complete record of pupil achievement — the result of collaboration between leading UK prep schools.

Rigour & balance

It empowers prep schools with rigorous academics and a balanced curriculum, promoting independent learning, technology, critical thinking, leadership and teamwork.

A guided journey

Aspiring schools start as partners and gradually integrate the PSB — beginning courses in Years 5 & 7, and awarding certificates in Years 6 & 8.
