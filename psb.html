What is the PSB?

An education that develops the whole child

The PSB is a programme of study that actively develops and tracks knowledge, skills and attitudes — preparing children academically, personally, socially, culturally and spiritually for the challenges of life as young adults.

With exit points at 11+ and 13+, it gives schools a richer, more complete record of achievement than results alone — recognised by senior schools alongside their own entrance assessments.

The focus

Life-long skills at the core

Adaptable thinkers

Develop flexible, adaptable intelligence — and bridge the skills gap a content-based curriculum can leave behind.

Confident communicators

Promote excellent communication and a genuine love of learning that challenges pupils' perceptions of school.

Real collaborators

Ensure collaboration and teamwork sit at the heart of learning, fostering application in every individual.

Membership

The benefits of joining the PSB

A supportive network

Draw on the experience of fellow PSB schools and teachers, plus dedicated support from our LST Consultants and Director of Schools.

Shared expertise

Annual subject meetings, online twilight sessions exploring pedagogy and best practice, an online bank of shared resources, and conferences designed to connect schools and share effective practice.

Ongoing development

Attendance at our annual conference and discounted training with partners — keeping teaching fresh and forward-looking.
