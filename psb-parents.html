For parents

A fuller picture of your child

The PSB prepares children for senior school and life — academically, personally, socially, culturally and spiritually — and celebrates the strengths a grade alone can’t show.

  • Recognises effort, character and growth, not just results
  • Builds confidence, independence and a love of learning
  • Gives you a clearer sense of how your child is really doing

"Children are encouraged, recognised and celebrated in their achievements — and directly prepared for the next step."

Interested in the PSB?

Our Director of Schools, Gregg Davies, is happy to talk through membership and how the PSB could work in your school.
