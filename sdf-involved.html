Help shape the next chapter

The SDF was developed collaboratively with a group of pilot schools, ensuring it was grounded in the realities of school life. While the framework provides a common structure and language, it is deliberately flexible and looks different in every setting, allowing schools to adapt it to their pupils, priorities and environment. Whether you're ready to take part or simply want to understand how it could work in your setting, we'd love to talk.
